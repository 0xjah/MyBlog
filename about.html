My story

I’m Ahmed, a 19-year-old cybersecurity enthusiast from Yemen, currently living in Jordan, born in 2005. My journey began with Capture the Flag (CTF) competitions, which honed my problem-solving skills and led to projects like a bootable Windows USB script, a method to hide APKs in pictures, and a terminal-based Snake game in Go. I proudly ranked first locally in Jordan on Hack The Box, enhancing my ethical hacking abilities. I use Arch Linux with Vim and the i3 window manager, embracing minimalism in my setup. I also enjoy anime, manga, and movies across all genres, which inspire my creativity. I'm excited to continue sharing my journey and projects in cybersecurity!

Services

I provide cybersecurity consulting, including vulnerability assessments and ethical hacking, as well as custom scripting for automation and simple game development. I offer technical support for Arch Linux setup, Vim, and i3 customization, along with educational workshops on cybersecurity and programming basics. Additionally, I’m available for freelance projects tailored to specific client needs.
